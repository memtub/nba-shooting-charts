How Pioneered Modern NBA Offenses

The Houston Rockets' offensive strategy has undergone significant transformations from 2000 to 2024, reflecting broader trends in NBA offensive philosophy and the team's changing personnel. In 2012, the arrival of Daryl Morey as General Manager dramatically transformed the Rockets' offensive approach. Embracing advanced analytics, the team pioneered the "Moreyball" strategy, which emphasized high-efficiency three-point shooting, shots at the rim, minimizing mid-range jumpers, and heavy use of pick-and-roll and isolation plays.
